# Modern & Contemporary Art — YouTube Series

## Series Overview
6 episodes covering major art movements from Neo-Classicism to Art Nouveau (~1760–1910). Each episode is driven by a central figure and a conflict, with the movement as backdrop. Tone: down-to-earth, storytelling-first, like telling a friend a story at 1 AM.

## Folder Structure
- `_series-wide/` — Shared assets, style guide, music, templates
- `ep01–06/` — Each episode contains: `research/`, `artworks/`, `visuals/`, `script/`, `production/`

## Episode Internal Structure (Template)
1. **Cold Open** (2–3 min) — Vivid scene, moment, quote
2. **"Okay, Let Me Back Up"** (5–8 min) — Historical context
3. **The Main Character** (10–15 min) — Biography as story
4. **The Work** (10–15 min) — Deep dives into key artworks
5. **The Legacy / Bridge** (3–5 min) — Hook into next episode
