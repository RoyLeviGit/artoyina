## Notes

- **Géricault self-portrait:** Multiple versions exist. Prefer the c. 1818–19 version — painted during the Raft period, most relevant visually.
- **Delacroix Morocco watercolors:** The Louvre holds most of these. The Wikimedia versions may be lower resolution. If quality is insufficient, the Louvre collections site (`collections.louvre.fr`) has better scans — search "Delacroix Maroc."
- **Saint-Sulpice murals:** The paintings are public domain but photo quality varies on Wikimedia. If the available shots have bad lighting or distortion, consider licensing one image from Getty — it is the only item in this episode that may require a license fee.
- **Ingres comparison:** `ingres_grande_odalisque_1814.jpg` already exists in `ep01-neoclassicism/artworks/`. No need to re-download — reference or copy it.
