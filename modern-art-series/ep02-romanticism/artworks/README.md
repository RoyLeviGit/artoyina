## Notes

- **Louvre paintings (Géricault + all Delacroix):** The Louvre open access portal gives the highest-quality scans. Prefer those over Wikimedia where available. Download from the "Download" button on each collection page — no account required.
- **Turner paintings:** National Gallery London open access for the three London works; MFA Boston for *The Slave Ship*. All are free for non-commercial use.
- **Friedrich:** The Kunsthalle Hamburg does not have a major open access program. The Wikimedia version (~5,000px wide) is sufficient for all uses including detail zooms.
- **Target resolution:** All main paintings should be at least 4,000px on the long edge for 4K detail zooms. Most Louvre downloads exceed this.
- **Detail crops:** Do not download separately. Crop in Photoshop or After Effects from the full-res masters.
