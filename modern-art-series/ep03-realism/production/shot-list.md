## ⚠️ Production Note: The Origin of the World

*The Origin of the World* (1866) is an explicit close-up of female genitalia — public domain, currently in the Musée d'Orsay. **Decide before editing:**
- Show uncensored (matches Courbet's intent; risky for YouTube)
- Show with a soft crop or partial blur (compromise)
- Reference verbally without showing (safest for platform, but loses the impact of the actual image)

Whichever choice: the image file is in `artworks/` and ready. The decision only affects how it's used in the edit.
