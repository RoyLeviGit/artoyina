## Notes

- **Courbet self-portrait** is low-res (1000px). Fine for a 2–3 second talking-head insert. If you need a larger version, the Musée Fabre may have a higher-res scan on request.
- **Bonheur portrait** is similarly low-res (735px). Same advice — brief insert only.
- **1848 barricades photograph:** This is one of the earliest surviving photographs of a revolutionary event anywhere. Worth a longer hold and a note in the script.
- **Vendôme Column:** The Disdéri photograph shows Commune officials posing amid the rubble — not the moment of the fall, but the aftermath. More historically interesting than an engraving.
