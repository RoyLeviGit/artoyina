## Notes
- **Van Gogh portrait:** The 1873 photograph is the only known adult image of Van Gogh. He was 19. Use it sparingly — it's the one document of what he actually looked like. For main "portrait" sequences, use one of his painted self-portraits instead (see `vangogh_self_portrait_1887.jpg` in artworks/).
- **Seurat portrait:** Low-res (1148px). Brief insert only.
- **Gauguin portrait:** The 1891 photograph is from around the time he first went to Tahiti — contextually appropriate.
- **Borinage:** No public-domain photographs of the Belgian Borinage coal mines in the 1870s appear to be on Wikimedia Commons. The Meunier painting is the best available alternative. Consider sourcing a period coal mining photograph from Getty/stock if the production budget allows.
- **Tahiti colonial photographs:** Not found on Wikimedia Commons. For the Tahiti section, the artworks themselves (Gauguin paintings) carry the visuals. Consider stock sources for a contextual shot of 1890s Tahiti if needed.
