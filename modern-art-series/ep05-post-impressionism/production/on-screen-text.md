## Notes
- All Van Gogh quotes are from his letters to Theo. The letters are public domain and available at vangoghletters.org
- "With an apple I will astonish Paris" is widely attributed to Cézanne but the exact source is uncertain. Do not present as a direct quote in lower third — use "attr." or present as part of narration.
- The Zola break (1886): Cézanne's response letter to Zola is extant but brief and polite. The script uses this correctly — the drama is in what was *not* said.
