## Silence Moments (Non-Negotiable)
1. Cold open letter reading — no underscore
2. The Starry Night first reveal — 10 seconds full silence
3. The break with Zola — near-silence
4. Van Gogh's death / unsent letter — near-silence

## Music Note — Gauguin Section
The Tahiti section must NOT be scored with lush, "paradise" music. The script is deliberately critical of Gauguin's colonial fantasy. Scoring it with exotic beauty would undermine the argument. Use something ambiguous, uneasy — beautiful but with something wrong underneath it.
