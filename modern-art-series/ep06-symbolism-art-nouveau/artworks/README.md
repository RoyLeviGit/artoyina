## Notes
- **Beethoven Frieze:** The Mittelwand (central wall) is the most important section — hostile forces, the knight, the embrace. Panoramic format (4920×1680). Pan slowly left to right in edit.
- **The Kiss:** Series emotional peak. Full 10-second silence — non-negotiable. Hold at full screen.
- **Mucha Job:** ~40MB. Download separately.
- **Ravenna mosaic:** Very large file (8768×12288). Used to illustrate the Byzantine gold-background technique that inspired Klimt's golden phase. Download separately.
- **Klimt Hygieia:** A detail/study — the only visual record of the Medicine figure Hygieia, as the full painting was destroyed. Use as insert in the University paintings scandal section.
- **Klimt Emilie Flögé portrait:** Tall and narrow format — crop to show the face and painted textile background. Used for the intimate death section.
- **University paintings (destroyed):** Do NOT include in this folder. Their B&W documentary photos are in `visuals/b-roll/` and labeled DESTROYED 1945.
