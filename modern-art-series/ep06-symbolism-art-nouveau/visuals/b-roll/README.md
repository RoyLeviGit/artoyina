## Notes
- **Moreau and Redon portraits:** Both are painted self-portraits, not photographs. Use briefly — the artworks carry more visual weight than the portraits for these artists.
- **Gaudí portrait (1878):** He was 26 in this photo. The only widely reproduced early portrait. Works for the Sagrada Família origin section.
- **Mucha portrait:** LCCN item from the Library of Congress — well-documented, reliable provenance.
- **University paintings:** Their absence and destruction is part of the story. Cut from the color/detail-rich earlier works to these B&W photos — the contrast is deliberate and should feel like loss.
