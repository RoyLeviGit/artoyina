## Notes
- **Water Lilies:** This is the Cleveland Museum's Agapanthus triptych panel (c.1915–26), one of the large-scale late works. Not an Orangerie panel (those are not on Wikimedia), but comparable in scale and style.
- **Renoir Moulin de la Galette:** Using the 2200px Wikimedia version. The Google Art Project version is 717 MB — impractical.
- **Haystacks:** All three are low-res from AIC/Wikimedia. Usable for 3-up side-by-side; not suitable for full-screen solo holds.
- **Giverny garden:** Only 1024px from Orsay. Brief supporting cut only — 4 seconds max.
- **Chevreul color science graphic:** Must be built in After Effects (explicitly scripted). Not downloadable. See `production/shot-list.md`.
