Music files are gitignored (too large). Get them from the shared drive or re-run the download process documented below.

## License Key

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **PD** | Public domain — composition and recording. No restrictions. |
| **PD-US** | Public domain in the US (pre-1928 publication or US gov't work). Use with confidence on YouTube. |
| **CC0** | Rights explicitly waived by rights holder (Musopen or equivalent). Functionally identical to PD. |
| **CC-BY** | Attribution required in video description. YouTube-safe. |
| **CC-BY-SA** | Attribution required + derivative works must share alike. YouTube-safe with attribution. |

> **YouTube note:** PD, PD-US, and CC0 tracks carry zero Content ID risk. CC-BY and CC-BY-SA tracks are legally safe but a small number may have been registered with Content ID by third parties — dispute any claim using the license URL as evidence.

## Re-downloading

All Wikimedia Commons tracks can be re-downloaded via:
```
curl -L -o {destination} "https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/{URL-encoded-filename}"
```

Original ep01 library tracks (beethoven, berlioz/mvt4, couperin, gluck, haydn, mozart, schubert, arina) should be sourced from `F:\artoyina\ep01\music\` on the Windows drive.
